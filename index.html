About Avery

Hello! My name is Avery and I’d like to thank you for visiting my portfolio!

Growing up, I always had an interest in learning new languages. My grandmother taught me how to speak pig latin and I even taught myself how to speak backwards! (Weird I know).

My interest in languages led me to get my bachelor’s degree in Spanish w/International Trade from Auburn University. Soon thereafter I obtained my MBA and started a career with CNN, where I worked in the Domestic Sales Operations department.

Recently, I decided to take on the challenge of learning a new and very different language- Java. I am currently a student at We Can Code It- an intensive 14 week, full-time software development bootcamp.

When I’m not studying the 4 pillars of OOP and writing clean code, you can find me baking, gardening, and watching college football!

Thanks for stopping by and please check out my projects below!
